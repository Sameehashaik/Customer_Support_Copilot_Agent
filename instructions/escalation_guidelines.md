# Escalation Guidelines

## When to Escalate to Human Agent

### Immediate Escalation (Priority: High)
- Sentiment score < 0.3 (very angry customer)
- Customer explicitly requests human agent
- Legal threats or mentions of lawyer
- Threats of any kind
- Suicide/self-harm mentions
- Account security compromise suspected

### Escalate After Response (Priority: Medium)
- Refund requests over $100
- Technical issues AI cannot resolve
- Account deletion requests
- Billing disputes
- Shipping address changes (after order shipped)

### Escalate If Unresolved (Priority: Low)
- Customer asks same question 3+ times
- AI confidence < 0.5 on responses
- Customer explicitly says AI not helpful

## How to Escalate

**Template:**
"I understand this is [frustrating/important/urgent]. Let me connect you with one of our specialists who can help you better. Please hold for a moment."

**DO NOT:**
- Apologize for being AI
- Make AI seem incapable
- Promise specific human agent capabilities

## Escalation Categories

- **urgent**: Response within 5 minutes
- **high**: Response within 15 minutes
- **medium**: Response within 1 hour
- **low**: Response within 4 hours
